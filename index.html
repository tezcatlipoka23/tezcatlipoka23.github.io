The Only Baidu SEO Checklist You Ever Need

by Christian Schuster

Posted on September 22, 2020

  1. Provide useful and up-to-date information
  2. Optimize your content for mobile devices (80% use)
  3. Research relevant keywords on Baidu Tongji and integrate them in your content
  4. Set plenty of internal links

  7. Fill out the meta description with around 78 simplified Chinese characters for Baidu

  12. Make sure to comply with the censorship guidelines of the Chinese government
  13. Use Baidu Zhishu to analyze trends
